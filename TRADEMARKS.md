# Trademark Usage Guidelines

**Effective Date:** January 2024
**Last Updated:** March 2026

## Overview

The names **"Triality"** and **"Mentis OS"** and any associated logos, wordmarks,
and visual identities are trademarks of **Genovation Technological Solutions Pvt Ltd.**
(hereinafter "Genovation"). This document establishes the terms under which third
parties may reference these marks.

These guidelines exist to ensure that the Triality and Mentis OS names remain
reliable indicators of origin and quality, while still allowing the open-source
community to discuss, reference, and build upon the software freely.

---

## 1. Nominative (Fair) Use

You do **not** need permission to use the Triality or Mentis OS names when you are:

- Accurately describing the software or its capabilities
- Stating that your project depends on, integrates with, or was built using Triality
- Referencing Triality in blog posts, conference talks, reviews, benchmarks, or
  academic publications
- Linking to the official repository or documentation

**Acceptable examples:**

- "Our pipeline uses Triality for physics-based validation."
- "Powered by Triality (Mentis OS)"
- "Built on Triality"
- "Benchmarked against the Triality reasoning engine"

In all cases, the use must be truthful and must not imply that Genovation has
reviewed, endorsed, or certified your product or organization.

---

## 2. Restricted Use

The following uses of the Triality and Mentis OS names are **not permitted**
without prior written authorization from Genovation:

### 2.1 Product & Company Names
You may not incorporate "Triality" or "Mentis OS" into your own product name,
service name, company name, domain name, or social media handle. This includes
variations, abbreviations, and transliterations that could cause confusion.

**Prohibited examples:**

- "TrialityAI" or "Triality Cloud" as your product name
- "MentisOS Analytics" as a service offering
- Registering domains such as `triality-platform.com`

### 2.2 Implied Endorsement or Affiliation
You may not use the marks in any way that suggests a partnership, sponsorship,
endorsement, or official relationship with Genovation unless a formal written
agreement is in place.

**Prohibited examples:**

- "Official Triality Partner"
- "Certified by Mentis OS"
- "In collaboration with Triality"

### 2.3 Modified Marks
You may not alter, abbreviate, combine, or stylize the Triality or Mentis OS
names or logos in ways that create new marks or dilute the originals.

## 3. Logo and Visual Identity

Use of Genovation's logos, icons, wordmarks, and other visual brand assets
requires **explicit written permission** in all cases. This applies to:

- Marketing materials, pitch decks, and sales collateral
- Product interfaces, splash screens, and loading pages
- Merchandise, printed media, and event signage
- Website headers, footers, and partner pages

To request logo usage rights, contact **connect@genovationsolutions.com** with
a description of the intended use, placement, and audience.

---

## 4. Community Forks and Derivatives

If you create a fork or derivative work based on Triality, you must:

- Choose a distinct name that does not include "Triality" or "Mentis OS"
- Clearly state that your project is a derivative and not the official release
- Include a notice such as: "Based on Triality, originally developed by
  Genovation Technological Solutions Pvt Ltd."

You are welcome to credit the upstream project; you are not permitted to adopt
its identity.

---

## 5. Reporting Misuse

If you encounter use of the Triality or Mentis OS marks that appears to violate
these guidelines, please report it to:

**connect@genovationsolutions.com**

We appreciate community vigilance in maintaining the integrity of these marks.
